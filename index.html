What is Enigma?

Enigma 5.0 is the fifth edition of CSI's flagship national-level hackathon — a 12-hour marathon of innovation, design, and code. Teams of undergraduate students come together to build working prototypes that solve real-world problems across two carefully curated tracks.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or a first-time hacker, Enigma is your arena. Mentors from the industry, workshops on cutting-edge tech, and a vibrant community await you.

Form a team of 4 members, pick your track, and build something extraordinary.
